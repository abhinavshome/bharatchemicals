About Us
We wish to introduce ourselves as the largest Thinners & Lacquers manufacturer in India Since 1985; our product range includes all kinds of thinners and lacquers to specialty products.

We as company have global vision, having presence in the national as well as the international arena. We have production facilities at Ghaziabad and are also in the process of expansion as per international standards. Our endeavor to provide the best product offering to our customers has resulted in a buildup of an impressive list of clientele. Our prestigious clients in India include the major leading brands in the market.

We provide complete solution to thinners & lacquers, supported by latest technology, intense R&D and vast experience in the industry. Our company has attained highest quality standards in all aspects.

Incepted in the year 2001 at Ghaziabad (Uttar Pradesh, India), we “Bharat Chemical Products” are a Sole Proprietorship firm engaged in Manufacturing and Supplying high quality range of Paver Coating Chemical, Wood Stain Colors, NC Lacquer, Paint Thinner, Sanding Sealer, Water Based PUD, PU Finish Coating, etc. We offer these products in excellent grade packaging options and at reasonable prices to our clients.
